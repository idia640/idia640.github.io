In our second week, we talked a bit about the nature of scientists and science. In this vein, how does one recognize a Paradigm shift? Perhaps, hindsight is the only test, but in a graceful redirection, Luca Turin says, "*You see, when you’re a scientist, you’re always selling ideas. And people are very resistant to ideas, and rightly so. Why should new ideas be accepted?*"
